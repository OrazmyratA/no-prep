No-Prep Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

No-Prep is an offline-first classroom material creation and game app for teachers and learners.

Data Collection

No-Prep does not collect, sell, or share personal data. The app does not require an account, login, analytics SDK, advertising SDK, or tracking SDK.

User Content

Topics, text, images, and audio created or imported by users are stored locally on the device. User content does not leave the device unless the user chooses to export or share a topic file.

Import And Export

Users can import and export topic files. Exported files are created only after the user requests an export, and users decide where to save or share those files.

Camera And Microphone

No-Prep may request camera access when the user chooses to take a photo, and microphone access when the user chooses to record audio. These permissions are used only for the selected action.

Internet Access

The app works offline for topic creation, import, export, and games. Internet access is used only when the user searches Pixabay for images.

Children

No-Prep is designed as a teacher and learner tool. It does not include accounts, chat, social features, ads, or personal data collection.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app developer at the support email listed on Google Play.
